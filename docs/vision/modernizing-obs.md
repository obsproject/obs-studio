# Modernizing OBS: A Strategic Vision for an AI-Native Streaming Platform

## Introduction: The Inflection Point for Real-Time Streaming

OBS Studio is a cornerstone of the modern streaming world, a testament to the power of open-source collaboration. However, the real-time media industry is now at a significant technological inflection point, driven by the convergence of ubiquitous AI and modern software architecture. The very definition of a "stream" is evolving from a simple video feed into an interactive, intelligent, and computationally intensive medium. This whitepaper outlines a comprehensive strategic vision for a modernized, AI-native streaming platform, built by strategically forking and evolving the OBS codebase to meet the challenges and opportunities of the next decade.
